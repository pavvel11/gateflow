How to Implement GateFlow Protection

Simple steps to add content protection to your pages

1

Add GateFlow Script

Include the GateFlow script at the bottom of your HTML page.

<script src="/api/gatekeeper?domain=yourdomain.com"></script>
2

Wrap Protected Content

Use the gateflow-protect class to mark content for protection.

<div class="gateflow-protect" data-product-slug="your-product">
3

Configure Product

Set up your product in the GateFlow admin panel with pricing and settings.
